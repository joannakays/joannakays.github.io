Hypnobirthing

The KG Hypnobirthing that I teach is a complete antenatal programme designed to help you give birth more gently, calmly and in greater comfort. It is simple, logical and profound. When taking a Hypnobirthing course you will learn how to release fear and build confidence during your pregnancy in order to have the best natural birth for you.

In order to get the best out of your birthing body you need to understand how it works and how you can work with it. Hypnobirthing helps you learn to trust your body, your instinct and your baby.
